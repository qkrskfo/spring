A.라이브러리다운로드후 summar.jar context/WEB-INF/lib 폴더에위치시킨다.(summer.jar) 
B. /WEB-INF/model2_controller_mapping.properties 파일생성 
C.모든클라이언트요청(.do)을 실행하도록 web.xml설정 
D.contoller설정파일위치파라메타등록 dispatcher com.itwill.summer.DispatcherServlet configFile /WEB-INF/model2_controller_mapping.properties 0 dispatcher *.do
	<servlet>
	  	<servlet-name>dispatcher</servlet-name>
	  	<servlet-class>com.itwill.summer.DispatcherServlet</servlet-class>
	  	<init-param>
	  		<param-name>configFile</param-name>
	  		<param-value>/WEB-INF/model2_controller_mapping.properties</param-value>
		</init-param>
		<load-on-startup>0</load-on-startup>
	</servlet>
	<servlet-mapping>
		<servlet-name>dispatcher</servlet-name>
		<url-pattern>*.do</url-pattern>
	</servlet-mapping>
